Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address when creating an account, and any financial data you choose to enter such as expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect sensitive financial information like credit card numbers or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of providing and improving our expense tracking services. Your expense data is used to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not use your personal financial information for advertising purposes or share it with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties. We may use trusted third-party service providers to help us operate our website and conduct our business, but these parties agree to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes specified.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can do this through your account settings or by contacting us. Please note that deleting your account will permanently remove all your expense data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to resolving any concerns you may have.
